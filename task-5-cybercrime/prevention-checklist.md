# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

•	Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts, including email, social media, and payment apps.

•	Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever available for extra security.

•	Never share OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or passwords with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or support team. 

•	Do not scan unknown QR codes or click suspicious payment links received through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.

•	Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID and name before sending money through apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm. 

•	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking, UPI transactions, or logging into important accounts. 

•	Regularly update apps and devices to protect against malware and security vulnerabilities. 

•	Check account statements and transaction history frequently to identify unauthorized activity early. 

•	Report suspicious activity immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in 

•	Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of online payment fraud or phishing incidents.
